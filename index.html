Inselect

Automated segmentation of scanned specimen images

View project on GitHub

Welcome to Inselect.

Inselect is a tool to support the mass digitisation of natural history specimens. It currently supports automated recognition, cropping and annotation of scanned images of items such as drawers of pinned insects and trays of microscope slides.

Installers for Mac OS X and for Windows can be downloaded from our releases page.
